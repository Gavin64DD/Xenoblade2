Basic Attacks

Every blade has a unqiue special and set of arts, specials have different affects like cause more damage, heal, or topple

The game will auto attack when you aren't move, or using specials/arts

Attacking will fill up a meter on the top left corner of the screen which when a bar is charged, you can revive down teamates by pressing A

Arts are basically the power moves that inflict more damage than specials do and are charged up using set specials

These arts also have elements which are used for primarly in blade combos

Blade Combos

There are multiple stages for every combo

Picture of Core Cystal Combos are from here

Level 1 Combos start whenever you or a party memeber uses any of your arts Activates at art level 1
Level 2 Any of the elements that combine with the element use, which is displayed right next to the art in use, will activate level 3 Activates at art level 2
Level 3 The final art that has to be used, any of the elements listed to the end of the combo chart will cause this attack Activates at art level 3

To help you find which attack to you next, a white border will be created around the element in the combo (from the picture it's stage 2 combo)

Doing these combos, having a full bars on that meter from before, and pressing + on your controller will cause a chain attack

This will cause you use arts again, but with more damage you'll notice an element symbol on the top screen

Breaking it will cause a burst allowing you to go around a second time and doing even more damage, you can repeat the process as many burst you get

Matching elements with their weakness cause the elements to break faster

Once an attack is caused another meter will come up on the top and you have to do your next attack before it depletes

Getting 4 burst will cause a final burst that causes you to an even stronger attack
