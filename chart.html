Leveling

There are 3 main ways to level up, they are in order of how much XP is earned

  1. Main Story Quests
  2. Side Quests
  3. Battling

All XP is immidatley given to you, besides with Side Quest which must be equiped by going into inn's and taking a rest

Equipment

There are five things to equip for drivers, and three for blades, they are listed below

Drivers

Upgrade Blades Upgrade blades attacks using points from battle
Engauge Blades Set blades you want to blades you want to battle with, you can have three per party member
Affinity Chart You gain seperate levels from battling and then cash in for spots on the chart which unlock different affects like higher defense, and attack
Accespots willsories Upgrades that cause different effects like increase health or damage, usually you can hold two per party member
Pouch Items Tempary upgrades, but more powerful than accesories, the more puches you obtain the more slots you get which you need to equip

Blades

Weapon Modification Depending on the upgrade, it can increase or decrease stats inclduing attack, critic hit, and defense
Affinity Chart Blades charts work differently from Driver charts, you do certain criteria to unloock on spots the chart, these can be use for field skills (which help you get to other places), attacks, and defense)
Aux Cores Basically the same thing as Accesories, but with different status upgrades
